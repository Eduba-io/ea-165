# Holland & Knight × Eduba landing page

One-page pitch built for Holland & Knight at eMerge Americas 2026, Booth 1176. Addresses the firm's innovation function, AI Practice and AI Policy & Regulation practice, Health AI Navigator, and the Miami office.

The masthead logo (`hklogo_full.png`, downloaded from `hklaw.com/-/media/images/global/hklogo_full.png`) is rendered on the firm's teal band, filtered to white to match the on-brand "white variant" the firm uses in dark contexts.

Default hosting path: `/for/holland-knight`.

Assets: `index.html`, `styles.css`, `logo.png`.
